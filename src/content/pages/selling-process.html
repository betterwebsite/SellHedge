How Much Could My Business Sell For?

When you start considering a sale, the first question is usually, ‘What can my business sell for?’ Valuation is always top of mind. At ExitHedge, I’ve worked with businesses across many industries and sizes, providing clear, practical assessments to help owners understand their market value. In a valuation, I’ll take into account:

  • Market Value: The price a business would reasonably sell for between a willing buyer and seller, with both having full knowledge and no pressure to act.
  • Intrinsic Value: The worth of a company based on fundamentals, often viewed from an investor’s perspective.
  • Fair Value: A standard used in legal settings to determine business worth.
  • Investment or Strategic Value: The unique value a business may hold for a specific buyer, such as a competitor or strategic partner.
